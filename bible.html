BAPTISM

The English word “Baptism” is a derivative of the Greek word “Baptisma (noun) or Baptizo (verb)” which is translated literally as “To immerse or dip something into something”. Biblically, Baptism means a person is dead for sin, and has resurrected again to walk in the newness of life found in Christ
